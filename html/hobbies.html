Hobbies


I have quite a few hobbies that I like to spend my time on when I'm not coding. Here they are in no particular order:

1) Knitting. I've been known to carry it around at times. I may or may not have been included in a comedian's comedy set because I was knitting. Currently, I'm knitting a large blanket that's the size of me. It's taking forever, but it's very pretty.

2) Reading. I love to read. I tend to read a lot of nonfiction, generally poetry or memoirs. Last year, I read 52 books and while the number was nice, I realized that I wanted to focus on specific subjects this year. I'm trying to read 25+ books from Black Women and Non-Binary Authors, a challenge brough on by the Free Black Women's Library.

3) Running. I used to hate to run. I like to say that now I just hate it less. I started working on interval running last year as a challenge and managed to increase my endurance by quite a bit. This was all thanks to the app Couch to 5K and my best friend Soph who ran with me and cheered me all the way.

4) Writing. I've been writing honest poetry since my early teens. Since then, it's apparently gotten better and I've started a poetry group with my best friend Soph called Spilt Red Wine Poetry. It has helped us connect others around the world and encourage honesty and vulnerability in these hard times.
